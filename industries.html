IBETO INDUSTRIES LTD

IBETO INDUSTRIES LTD

was incorporated in 1984 to serve the Nigerian public in their quest for colour print photographic processing. With an array of modern and sophisticated machines, it has been able to meet the needs of its numerous customers in both quality and service through its network of branches all over Nigeria.

In keeping with its diversification policy, the Company established in 1998 a modern Liquefied Petroleum Gas (Butane) Plant located at Nnewi for the distribution of industrial and domestic gas to its teeming customers.
